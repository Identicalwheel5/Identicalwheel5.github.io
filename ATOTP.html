A Tail of Two Pawrtners

You're trapped in a hauntingly silent animal pound, controlling not one, but two unlikely heroes: a spirited dog and a mischievous ghost cat. Their fates are intertwined, and escape depends on your ability to master their unique abilities. Feel the dog's eager paws with WASD, while the cat glides through walls with the mouse, revealing hidden secrets. The clock is ticking, and whispers of past inhabitants echo through the halls. Can you forge an unbreakable bond, solve the pound's cryptic puzzles, and lead them to freedom?
